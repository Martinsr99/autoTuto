Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how AutoTuto handles information in relation to its video automation services. AutoTuto is a private, personal-use application and does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

AutoTuto does not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal information from users
  • Browsing history or cookies
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • User behavior data
  • Any information from third parties

3. Authentication and API Access

The application uses authentication tokens to interact with third-party services:

  • TikTok API: Authentication tokens are used solely to upload videos to the owner's TikTok account
  • YouTube API: OAuth tokens are used solely to upload videos to the owner's YouTube channel
  • Google Drive API: Tokens are used to detect and download new videos from the owner's Drive

All authentication tokens are:

  • Stored locally on the owner's machine
  • Never transmitted to any third party
  • Used exclusively for the owner's accounts
  • Not shared, sold, or distributed

4. Data Processing

Video processing occurs entirely on the owner's local infrastructure:

  • Videos are processed using FFmpeg locally
  • No video content is sent to external servers (except the target platforms: TikTok/YouTube)
  • Temporary files are automatically deleted after processing

5. Third-Party Services

This application interacts with the following third-party platforms:

  • TikTok: For uploading video content
  • YouTube: For uploading video content
  • Google Drive: For detecting new video files

Each of these services has their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

6. No Commercial Use

This application is for personal use only and does not:

  • Collect data for commercial purposes
  • Sell or share any information
  • Use data for advertising or marketing
  • Create user profiles

7. Security

Security measures in place:

  • All credentials are stored in environment variables
  • Credentials are excluded from version control (.gitignore)
  • Docker containers are isolated
  • No external access to the system

8. Data Retention

AutoTuto does not retain any user data. All processing is temporary:

  • Videos are processed and immediately uploaded
  • Temporary files are deleted after processing
  • No logs containing personal information are kept

9. Your Rights

Since AutoTuto is a private tool that does not collect personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
  • The owner has full control over their own credentials
  • No third-party data is processed

10. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. No information is knowingly collected from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

12. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the repository owner through GitHub at: https://github.com/Martinsr99/autoTuto

13. Compliance

This application complies with:

  • TikTok Developer Terms of Service
  • YouTube API Terms of Service
  • Google API Terms of Service
  • Applicable data protection regulations
